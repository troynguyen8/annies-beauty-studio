About Us

Annie’s Beauty Studio is a private and sophisticated studio, delivering quality nails care, skin care, hair removal and microblading, and permanent makeup. Our studio dedicated to consistently providing high customers satisfaction by rendering excellent service, professional technique, quality products as well as a relaxed and friendly atmosphere.

To truly achieve the status of the Beauty Experts in the field of Cosmetology, it all starts with passion. I’m passionate about the quality of my work and also thrive on providing you with assistance recommendations to enhance your beauty.

The main goal of my studio is making sure your comfort, beauty and care remain the top of my priority. You can rest assured that you can achieved the best beauty services at my studio.

In order to stay on top of my profession, I have gone through training in the United States as well as from other industry leaders from countries overseas. I have been and will continue attending conferences and events all year round.

The beauty industry never stops evolving, so I am continuing to strive to familiarize myself with the latest beauty trends.
