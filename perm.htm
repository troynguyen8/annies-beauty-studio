Microblading NE Permanent Makeup


My eyebrow method is a tattoo done by hand and not machine which is called microblading, also known as eyebrow embroidery.

With this method I can create almost any look, from a very natural light finish or a beautifully bold brow.

Microblading is great not just for anyone looking to improve or enhance the appearance of their brows, but also for those with fine and gappy brows, and even those suffering from alopecia, as sparse or missing brows can be completely restored. The strokes are more visible when there is high contrast between skin and ink color, and they can last 2-3 years depending on the skin type and color chosen.

For eyeliner itself can be very thin or as thick as your own natural lashes and does not have a flick or extended tip. This is just to fill in between the lashes to create the illusion of thicker, darker, and completely full lashes without looking lined or like obvious make-up.

This can be done just on top or the bottom or on both lashes. No one will know you've had anything done but your eyes will be more permanently defined and gorgeous.

Lash enhancement is great for everyone, especially those with really gappy or sparse lashes as thicker lashes accentuate the beauty of the eyes and make them look more youthful.

For lips, I offer lip-blush tattoo, this is to define the edges of the lips and to create a better contour and symmetry. I only use the most natural pigments as this to fake a fuller lip naturally, the colour is most concentrated around the border then ombre's into the center of the lips to blend seamlessly with the colour of your own lips.

Great for those who have lost colour around the edges of their lips or who have really pale, colourless lips or who will sometimes illuminate the need for lip fillers. Lip blush tattoo can last 3-5 years.
